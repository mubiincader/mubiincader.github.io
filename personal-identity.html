Personal Identity.

The logo is made from a geometric font, named Konnect. The main justification for this is that the basic shapes from which it was made is supposed to convey design, thus the use of a geometric type for the logo.

The little yellow circle is supposed to have the meaning of solution for any kind of problem any customer is facing.

As a whole, the logo conveys the message that even the basic designs can be the best solution.
